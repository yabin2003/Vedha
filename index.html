Latex Mattress

Latex foam is a 100% natural, eco-friendly product that resists dust, bacteria, and odors, ensuring an allergy-free sleep. Harvested from sustainably managed forests, it is made exclusively from rubber tree sap using the time-tested Dunlop process, making it one of the greenest mattresses available.

Why Choose VEDHA Latex Mattress?

Toxin Free

Latex foam is a 100% natural component obtained from the sap of rubber tree, hence, it is free of toxins, Natural latex do not emit carcinogenic substances.

Highly Breathable

Latex foam has an open cell-structure which regulates air flow like no other mattress foam, it keeps the surface temperature cool even during hot and humid condition.

Durable

The durability of a natural latex mattress is unmatched, as it can last for as long as 20 years or even more. Other mattresses can live only up to 5 years.

Safe for Children

Latex mattress is completely compatible with children. The firmness provides the right support to your child and delivers a dreamy slumber.

Zero Partner Disturbance

Modern Latex Mattress are equipped with the feature of no motion transfer. It locks motion and doesn't allow it to transfer to the other side of the bed.

Hypoallergenic

There is no room for bed bugs and other irritating bacteria on the latex mattress. Latex is antimicrobial, and therefore, is naturally mold and dust mite resistant.
